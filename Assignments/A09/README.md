# A09 – Autonomous Agents in Industry 4.0

## Objective
The purpose of this assignment was to analyze a case study on autonomous agents and understand their role in Industry 4.0 systems.

## Case Study Overview
The study explores the use of autonomous agents in Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) within industrial environments. These agents are capable of making decisions, learning from experience, and adapting to changing conditions.

## Implementation
- Multi-Agent System (MAS)
- Reinforcement Learning (Double Deep Q-Network - DDQN)
- 2D simulation environment
- Agents perform tasks such as:
  - Material transportation
  - Battery management
  - Path optimization

## Key Benefits
- High efficiency in task completion
- No collisions or system failures during testing
- Flexibility compared to traditional AGV systems
- Improved decision-making through learning

## Challenges
- Simulation-based testing (not real-world validated)
- Training complexity and instability
- Limited scalability across different environments

## Future Implications
Autonomous agents have the potential to revolutionize Industry 4.0 by enabling smarter, decentralized, and adaptive industrial systems.

## What I Learned
This assignment helped me understand how reinforcement learning can be applied to real-world industrial problems. It also showed me the gap between simulation and real-world deployment.
